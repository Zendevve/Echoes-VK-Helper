Echoes Vulkan Helper is released under the **GNU Affero General Public License v3.0**
(AGPL-3.0). AGPL lets you read, modify, and redistribute the code, but anything you
run as a network service must also be published under AGPL — including your
modifications.

If you want to keep your changes closed, **or** offer a hosted version without
disclosing your source, you need a commercial license.

## When you need this

- You want to ship a modified version of the wizard to your own users without
  publishing your changes.
- You want to offer a hosted or managed variant of the tool.
- You want to bundle it with another commercial product.
- Your company policy forbids AGPL-licensed code.

## What you get

- A standalone commercial license for the version you fork, signed by the
  project owner.
- No requirement to publish your modifications.
- Perpetual for the licensed version (no recurring fee per build).
- Optional priority support on a separate agreement.

## What it does **not** cover

- The bundled [DXVK](https://github.com/doitsujin/dxvk) project. DXVK is MIT
  and remains under its own license.
- The PyInstaller runtime. PyInstaller is GPL-2.0 with a bootloader exception —
  see their license for redistribution terms.
- Any third-party assets you add.
